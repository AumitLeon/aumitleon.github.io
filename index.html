My name is Aumit Leon, and I am a Software Engineer at Indigo Ag. My team builds microservices within the Marketplace Platform, faciliating high throughput transactions and supporting various Marketplace application features.

In February of 2020, I graduated from Middlebury College where I studied Computer Science and Mathematics. In the Spring of 2019, I completed my undergraduate thesis, titled Accelerating Deep Neural Networks. I was and continue to be interested in scalable machine learning within production systems, an idea proposed in my Senior Seminar at Middlebury College, where I began work on ArchMLP.

In the past I've contracted with Staple Health to develop machine learning algorithms to detect medical non-adherence while helping to build out databases used as input into predictive models.
